The Best Movies According To ME

My Top 3 picks of the Month

Set It up

This movie has a beautiful storyline. 2 assistants working overshits handling their workaholic bosses decide to set them up so they could get hold of their own lives. As the story goes Male lead discover the purpose of his life from the female lead who is extravagently talkative but at the same time witty and street smart.

My Name

My name is a series about a girl taking revenge of her father's death. To achieve this she joins up with thw mafia boss. But as the series goes on the truth unfolds itself in an unfamiliar way for her. The series has a magnificient plot with good action scences and sense of justice.

Meet Joe BLack

This movie has a fictional connection as a devil takes over a human body to watch the last moments of an old man and experience a human life. As the story moves forward, the non-human understands much of the human world and even falls in love. But as the final date of death of the old rich man arrives, he has to leave with him, letting go of all the things he found in the new world.
